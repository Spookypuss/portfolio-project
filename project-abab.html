ABAB home brewery website

Starting in 2016, ABAB grew out of a couple of physical scientists bringing their expertise in laboratories around the world to bear fruit in the home brewery.

For years I'd been creating beers and their labels, and sharing these with my friends and family. I wanted to break out of the physical lab-book that archives ABAB's creations, and share with even the remotest of our fans. So, in 2021 while getting a taste for Figma, it was the perfect design to play around with: the ABAB home brewery website.

As logically as it came to mind to create a design for the website, it naturally followed that when I was playing around with web development I reached for those designs to try my hand at translating them to the web. This site is my first attempt at a functional, responsive website, with real users in mind. User testing was also a simple case of sharing the url with friends and watching the feedback roll in.

Skills used
